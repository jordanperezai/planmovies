# Genesis

> Session 1 | 2026-05-25 | Built the whole thing from scratch in one session.

---

Started with a 42KB HTML file that stored RSVPs in localStorage. Ended with a live platform at planmovies.com backed by Supabase, deployed on Cloudflare, monitored by a Worker, alerting via Telegram, collecting payments through Stripe, and scaffolded with the full Zordon framework.

The session had a clear arc. The first hour was infrastructure: Supabase tables, Cloudflare Pages, wiring the backend. The middle was content: researching the movie, pulling trailers, finding Reddit sentiment, writing copy. The last stretch was refinement: the news ticker, the seat map, payment flow, and the morph.

---

The copy went through five iterations. "25 operatives. One mission." was the military theme. "Recliners" was the low point. "Spielberg. UFOs. Opening night." is where it landed. The Reddit research shaped everything. The word "believe" kept surfacing across every community. The emotional register isn't hype. It's anticipation laced with something heavier. That distinction drove every copy decision after.

The "seven billion" detail was a turning point. Jordan caught it before I did. The trailer says seven, the world has eight. One billion aren't human. That detail alone justifies the breaking news ticker. The movie is blurring into reality, and the app leans into that.

---

The architecture decisions were practical, not aspirational. Cloudflare over Vercel because Browser Rendering exists and might matter for ticket day. Supabase because the MCP was already connected. Single HTML file because a framework would have tripled the build time for zero user-facing benefit. Stripe because Venmo doesn't give you a checkout page.

The NJ ticket bot statute was a surprise. Fourth-degree crime, up to 18 months. Changed the automation strategy from "build a bot" to "build an alert system and buy manually." The right call.

---

The one thing that hasn't happened yet: nobody outside this session has seen the app. The link hasn't been shared. That's the gap between building and shipping. Everything we built today is potential energy. It converts the moment Jordan texts the link to the family chat.
