Red is the most crowded color in screen entertainment. Netflix + Cinemark + AMC. Gold is the only open lane — and it's the prestige-film signal.

My read. The honest tension: red feels right, gold is ownable. Bright red (B) is out — it's Netflix camouflage. The real choice is gold (A) — the only lane nobody owns, premium, already live — versus velvet red (C) — the warmth of the theater without looking like a streamer.

If the goal is "stand out in the category," gold. If the goal is "feel like the movies," velvet red. Either one is defensible; bright red isn't. Pick the bet — I build the row in that color as the full system.
