Verified by Shadeed, product-09. cadence: frozen. last-confirmed: 2026-05-28
- **No single mark spans 16-64px; ship a simplified small glyph (favicon/nav) + a fuller logo (≥40px).** Context: Johnson + Shadeed, product-09 — at 16px the seat's armrests collapse to <3px slivers and the cut tapers to nothing. - **For an aging/mixed first-contact audience, carry meaning in SOLID/positive form, not absence.** Context: Johnson, product-09 — a small internal dark wedge reads as a defect before a symbol; reduced contrast sensitivity attacks thin internal edges hardest. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-28
