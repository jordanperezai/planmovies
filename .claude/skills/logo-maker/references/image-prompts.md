## Round 2 — research-informed (2026-05-28)

Round 1 read amateur: gradient glow, clip-art dot-faces, thin outline, three ideas at once. Pro principles (LogoLounge 2025 trend report, app-icon best-practice 2025/26): ONE single idea, flat (no gradients/glow/faces), negative space does the work (FedEx arrow = the "aha"), distinctive silhouette that survives at 16px, 2-3 colors max. Each prompt below = one distinct professional technique.

1. **Negative space (FedEx school):** A single bold gold speech bubble; its interior negative space forms a clean play triangle. One shape, one hidden meaning. No gradient/glow/faces.
2. **Monoline, one continuous stroke (Airbnb school):** One even-weight gold line drawing a bubble that resolves into a play triangle, drawn without lifting the pen.
3. **Solid figure-ground, audience from behind:** Filled gold bubble; knocked out = 3 rounded heads seen FROM BEHIND facing a bright bar (screen). No outlines, no gradient, no faces. "Watching together."
4. **Custom monogram glyph:** A gold "P" whose bowl/counter doubles as a play button. One ownable glyph.
5. **Modern minimal emblem:** A thin gold ring containing a single bold play-bubble mark. Contemporary film-society seal, disciplined, NOT vintage/distressed.
6. **The row, from behind:** 3 bold gold rounded shapes (backs of people / plush seats) under one bright horizontal bar (screen). Solid shapes, no faces, no gradient.

Amateur tells to forbid in every prompt: gradients, glow/bloom, drop shadows, realistic or dot-faces, thin hairline outlines, more than one core idea.
