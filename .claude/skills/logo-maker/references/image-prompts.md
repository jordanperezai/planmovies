## Round 2 — research-informed (2026-05-28)

Round 1 read amateur: gradient glow, clip-art dot-faces, thin outline, three ideas at once. Pro principles (LogoLounge 2025 trend report, app-icon best-practice 2025/26): ONE single idea, flat (no gradients/glow/faces), negative space does the work (FedEx arrow = the "aha"), distinctive silhouette that survives at 16px, 2-3 colors max. Each prompt below = one distinct professional technique.

1. **Negative space (FedEx school):** A single bold gold speech bubble; its interior negative space forms a clean play triangle. One shape, one hidden meaning. No gradient/glow/faces.
2. **Monoline, one continuous stroke (Airbnb school):** One even-weight gold line drawing a bubble that resolves into a play triangle, drawn without lifting the pen.
3. **Solid figure-ground, audience from behind:** Filled gold bubble; knocked out = 3 rounded heads seen FROM BEHIND facing a bright bar (screen). No outlines, no gradient, no faces. "Watching together."
4. **Custom monogram glyph:** A gold "P" whose bowl/counter doubles as a play button. One ownable glyph.
5. **Modern minimal emblem:** A thin gold ring containing a single bold play-bubble mark. Contemporary film-society seal, disciplined, NOT vintage/distressed.
6. **The row, from behind:** 3 bold gold rounded shapes (backs of people / plush seats) under one bright horizontal bar (screen). Solid shapes, no faces, no gradient.

Amateur tells to forbid in every prompt: gradients, glow/bloom, drop shadows, realistic or dot-faces, thin hairline outlines, more than one core idea.

## Round 4 — THE RED ROW (current direction, 2026-05-28)

Direction pivoted off gold. Jordan: "red, movies, night — it resonates the most." The winning feeling is the
screenshot `Screenshot 2026-05-28 at 8.32.38 PM.png`: deep cinema-red seats in a row, the CENTER seat lit like a
spotlight, floating in near-black with a low red glow bleeding up from below (screen-light on the seats). Concept =
**the row of three seats, center = "you."** Palette: deep red `#cc2b2b` (base), shadowed red `#9e2025` (side seats),
hot lit red `#f0584c` (center seat glow), near-black `#06070a`, white type.

Two jobs, two prompt types. Generate BOTH:
- **(A) the lit/dimensional version** = the feeling Jordan loves → becomes the app-icon tile + hero art.
- **(B) the FLAT version** = clean, single-color, isolated, generous margin → this is the one logo-maker TRACES
  into production SVG (favicon/nav). Without a flat version there is nothing clean to trace.

### Shared rules
- No text, no words, no letters. One centered subject. Square 1:1.
- Seats are PLUSH and LEGLESS (rounded back + cushion, no thin legs — legs look broken small).
- Three seats in a row, the MIDDLE one brighter/lit = "you." The ROW is the mark at EVERY size incl. favicon — do
  NOT reduce to a single seat (Jordan's call 2026-05-28: "the favicon should still be the main logo. the three seats
  with the glowing one in the middle is what makes the logo"). A single seat reads "lonely" — rejected. At 16px the
  glow can't render, so the lit middle = a brighter SOLID red middle seat vs deeper solid side seats (value contrast).
- Forbid: gradients-as-texture-noise, lens flare clutter, faces, people, popcorn, film reels, clapperboards, tickets,
  marquees, any text, busy backgrounds.

### (A) Lit / dimensional — the app-icon & hero feeling
> A premium app icon. Three plush cinema seats in a row seen from the front, simplified rounded silhouettes with no
> legs. Deep cinematic red. The middle seat is lit like a spotlight is on it, glowing a hotter red; the two outer
> seats sit in shadow. Everything floats in a near-black void with a soft red glow rising from below, like screen-light
> spilling onto the seats in a dark theater. Moody, cinematic, the hush right before the movie starts. Single centered
> subject, generous padding, no text, 1:1.

Variations (change ONE thing): • single lit seat only (favicon hero) • wider 5-seat row, middle lit • the row seen
slightly from above • warmer ember glow vs cooler deep red • subtle volumetric beam of light hitting the center seat.

### (B) Flat / traceable — the production logo
> A flat 2D vector logo, minimal and iconic. Three plush cinema seats in a row, front view, simplified solid
> silhouettes with rounded backs and cushions, NO legs. Solid single-color red (#cc2b2b) shapes on a plain near-black
> background. The middle seat solid-filled, the two outer seats drawn as clean thick outlines of the same red. Bold,
> high-contrast, evenly weighted, geometric, readable at 16px. Centered, lots of empty margin around it. No gradient,
> no glow, no shadow, no 3D, no text. Square 1:1.

Variations (change ONE thing): • all three solid (no outlines) • single solid seat (favicon) • on a dark rounded-square
tile • slightly wider/tighter seat proportions • on pure white background (to check the unfurl).
