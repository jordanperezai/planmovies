## Validated Principles

- **"You're #N on the crew" is the strongest social mechanic across all ages.** Context: Session 01. Marco screenshots it. Lisa checks it for family names. Carmen feels accomplished seeing herself and Jordan. Ray uses it as his receipt. Sofia gets early-adopter energy. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **The confirmation step is the best step — zero confusion across all ages.** Context: Session 01. The only part of the flow that all 5 personas rated positively without qualification. "You're in, [name]!" with the crew list is universally satisfying. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **Form labels under 14px lose Carmen and Ray.** Context: Session 01. At 10px uppercase monospace, both personas were squinting. Johnson previously flagged 11px minimum — persona session confirms 14px is the practical floor for this audience. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **Selected pill needs solid fill, not just outline, for 60+ users.** Context: Session 01. Carmen couldn't tell at arm's length whether her selection registered with just an outline. Solid gold background removed all ambiguity. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **Plain numbers (1, 2, 3) beat "Just Me / +1 / +2" for party size.** Context: Session 01. Carmen and Ray both expressed confusion about "+1 including or excluding me?" Plain numbers have no ambiguity. cadence: frozen. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **Nobody under 35 uses share buttons. They screenshot and text.** Context: Session 01. Marco and Sofia both said this independently. Share buttons serve Carmen and older users. The buttons still earn their place, but the primary sharing mechanic for young users is the screenshot. cadence: stable. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
- **Showing ticket price and showtime reduces commitment anxiety for Lisa and Carmen.** Context: Session 01. Lisa said unknown cost creates anxiety ("I'd want to know the price before I tap"). Carmen wanted to know if RSVP secured a ticket or if a separate step remained. Adding "~$15" and clarification line ("tickets are separate") resolved both. cadence: tactical. last-confirmed: 2026-05-27
