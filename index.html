What is Disclosure Day?
A new Steven Spielberg film about a small-town family who witnesses something in the sky that changes everything. Spielberg's first UFO movie since Close Encounters (1977). Written by David Koepp. Score by John Williams at 93.
Why opening night?
Some movies you see whenever. This one is a first-night-together movie. The whole crew, same theater, same row, same reaction. That's the point.
Do I need to know anything about UFOs?
No. Spielberg makes movies for everyone. But if you're curious, scroll down. There's a reason this film is coming out now.
